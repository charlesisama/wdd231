Port Harcourt Chamber of Commerce

Site Plan

Site Purpose

The Port Harcourt Chamber of Commerce website promotes local businesses, encourages networking between entrepreneurs, and provides information about community events and business opportunities.

Target Audience

Local business owners looking to network and grow their businesses.

Entrepreneurs seeking information about the business environment in Port Harcourt.

Visitors or investors interested in exploring business opportunities in the city.

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #1B4965
  • Secondary Color: #62B6CB
  • Background Color: #F4F4F4
  • Text Color: #333333

Typography

  • Headings Font: Poppins
  • Body Font: Inter

Site Map

  • Home
  • Discover
  • Directory
  • Join

Wireframes

Desktop and mobile layouts follow the chamber wireframe provided in the assignment.
